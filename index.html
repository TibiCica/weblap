Gamemodes

You can play this game in five different modes giving you five different ways to experience this game:

  • Survival: Survival mode is the core Minecraft experience where players must gather resources, manage health, improve your gears, and fend off dangers to thrive in a challenging environment.
  • Creative: Creative mode is a sandbox experience where players have unlimited resources and no survival constraints, allowing for pure creativity and exploration. Fly freely across the world to build massive structures or explore without limits.
  • Hardcore: Hardcore mode is the ultimate survival challenge for experienced players who want a high-stakes adventure.
  • Adventure: Adventure mode is designed for custom maps and gameplay experiences where players interact with the world in controlled ways.
  • Spectator: Spectator mode is available on Java only. This gamemode gives you the possibility to go through walls and watch over the events without interacting with your surroundings
